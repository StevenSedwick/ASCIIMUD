ASCIIMUD — configuration

Point the Twitch extension at your Cloudflare Worker EBS. The companion app on your PC HMAC-signs every snapshot and POSTs to that URL; viewers' browsers connect over WebSocket and receive a fan-out of the same data.

No trailing slash. The viewer will connect to wss://<host>/ws/<your-channel-id> automatically.
HMAC secret rotation (advanced)

The HMAC shared secret is configured directly in your Worker (wrangler kv:key put SECRETS secret:<channel_id> <hex>) and in your local companion/config.toml's [twitch] section. Rotating it is a two-step deploy; this panel does not store it for security reasons.
